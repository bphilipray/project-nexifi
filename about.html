Nexifi : Where we are now

Nexifi is the evolution of the traditional Piso WiFi model—designed for modern entrepreneurs, communities, and tech-savvy users who seek more than just connectivity. Born from the legacy of Adopisoft, the pioneering Piso WiFi system in the Philippines, Nexifi redefines what's possible with hotspot technology.

With Nexifi, you don’t just offer internet access—you unlock multiple income streams. From ad revenue sharing and digital services to smart system integrations and future-ready features, Nexifi empowers hotspot owners to scale their earnings beyond coin-based sessions
